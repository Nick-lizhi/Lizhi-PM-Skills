## Skills

### [Lizhi-PM-Review](./Lizhi-PM-Review/)

**产品判断力引擎** · Product Judgment Engine

输入任意材料（文档、报告、方案、数据、会议材料），输出从理解到行动的完整判断。覆盖把控、审定、辅导三种意图，内置七个独立分析框架。

Feed any material (docs, reports, proposals, data, meeting notes) and get a complete judgment from understanding to action. Covers three intents — monitor, approve, coach — with seven built-in analysis frameworks.
